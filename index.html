Proposal Story

In December of 2023 Ivan took Jenna out to dinner for a last night of peace and quiet before they went up and celebrated the holidays with their families. The idea of going out to dinner was so spontaneous and exciting, Jenna didn’t think anything of it! With the reservations Jenna and Ivan walked up to NOBU in Newport Beach, the same place they started the year for Jenna’s birthday that January. With Coffees in their hands, the hosts said yes they were available right away and escorted them to a table.

The whole dinner was so much fun! Right before dessert, Ivan escorted himself to the restroom and his coat was thrown on the chair, so Jenna went to pick it up and place it nicely over the chair. As she picks it up and places it on the chair, it dawns on her that it could be the night. She picks up the jacket again and “smooths it out” Feeling nothing she places it back on the chair and doesn’t think about it again. Dinner was so early Jenna and Ivan wanted to keep enjoying the night after it, Ivan suggested a nice walk on the beach. On the way to the beach Jenna was enjoying all the Holiday lights and just being in the fun date night moment.

They get to Crystal Cove in Newport Beach, walk over to the overlook cliff and under the bright moon enjoying the sounds of the ocean Ivan call Jenna’s name a couple of times because she was enjoying the view. She looked over and he was on one knee. He was nervous, she was freaking out and in the end she said YES! As he was placing the ring on, she tells him, wrong hand! Trying to take it off it falls to the ground and everyone holds their breath and gasps. Feww, it did not fall threw the wooden boards He picks it up and places it on the correct hand. As they walk back to the car she stops and says, “wait did I tell you yes?” They laugh and the rests is history.
